GeoCam Viewer - Phone Photo Localization

Demo

How to Use

  1. Click the phone camera icon in the top-right corner of the viewer
  2. Upload a phone photo by dragging it onto the dropzone or clicking to browse
  3. Adjust the estimated FOV and Facing values if needed
  4. Click "Localize in Panorama" to find the viewpoint
  5. The viewer will navigate to the matched position and show results
